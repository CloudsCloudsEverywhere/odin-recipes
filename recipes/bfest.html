Breakfast Smoothie

For a quick breakfast to throw together, smoothies are the go-to! Made with frozen fruit, yogurt, almond butter, oats and orange juice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries
  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon of Almond butter
  • 1/4 cup of greek yogurt
  • orange juice

Steps:

  1. Combine the fruits, yogurt and almond butter in a blender.
  2. Add orange juice till its almost covering the ingredients.
  3. Blend on high while using a spoon to stop any blockage until desired consistence.
  4. Enjoy!
