Chicken with rice and veggies

Description:

A simple healthy dinner composed of oven roasted chicken with roasted veggies and a side of rice.

Ingredients:

  • about a 0.75lbs of chicken breast, trimmed and cut.
  • 1 white potatoe, washed and diced
  • 1 cup of broccoli florrets
  • 1 bell pepper,cut into strips
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Set your oven to 425F
  2. Line two oven safe trays with aluminum foil and spray them down with Vegitable oil.
  3. Gather your veggies and then sliced the pepper into strips
  4. Afterwards, cut the potato into about 1/2 inch chunks.
  5. Combine your veggies in a bowl and toss with Olive oil, then salt and pepper.Afterwards, put them on one of the trays and set it aside.
  6. Grab your chicken breast and make sure the fat is trimmed off and the bones are removed. Cut the breast into three somewhat equal parts and flatten the pieces out a little with your hand or the knife.Rub EVOO on them, then salt and pepper. Set it on the other tray.
  7. Grab the rice and wash the starch off while having the water boiling. Add some salt the water, about 1/4 teaspoon.When the water is ready, add the rice and cover it. Reduce heat to low and set a timer for 15 minutes.
  8. Put the veggies and chicken in the oven and set a timer for 20 minutes.I would clean up and doom scroll while I wait.
  9. When rice is done, fluff it and put it aside to cool. Then when 20 minutes is up, remove the chicken and set a timer for 10 minutes. Make sure the chickens temp is 165F.
  10. After the 10 minutes, pull the veggies out. You should be able to stick a fork in the potatoes very easily. Combine the them with the rice on bottom, then veggies and topped with the chicken!
